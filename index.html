OUR APPROACH

�

At Mphasis, engineering is in our DNA. We lead with design and architecture to deliver a portfolio of next-generation offerings and services that blend our deep domain expertise with cutting-edge technology. As an AI-led, platform-driven company with human-in-the-loop intelligence, we help global enterprises modernize, infuse AI, and scale with agility. The Mphasis.ai unit and our AI-powered ‘Tribes’ are focused on client outcomes and embed artificial intelligence and autonomy into every layer of the enterprise technology and process stack.

�

We believe that ‘AI Without Intelligence Is Artificial™ ' and built on this belief is Mphasis NeoIP™,our breakthrough AI platform, that orchestrates a powerful mix of Mphasis-built solutions, partner technologies, and client assets within a single platform. Powered by Ontosphere - a dynamic and ever-evolving knowledge base, the platform drives intelligent engineering to deliver compounding value, enable continuous transformation, and ensure that AI-driven change is fast, accurate, and aligned with long-term business goals.

�

At the heart of our approach is customer-centricity, which is reflected in our proprietary Front2Back™ transformation framework. The framework uses the exponential power of cloud and cognitive to deliver hyper-personalized digital experiences and drive customer-centric transformation. Leveraging domain-contextualized offerings that are driven by deep domain expertise and our cross-functional teams that uphold the Mphasis Way of engagement, we help clients build and scale faster.

At Mphasis, we thrive on innovation. We turn ideas into action, delivering solutions like the Neo series to help enterprises stay efficient and competitive in an AI-driven world.
